Car Import Services

Brandon Motors makes importing your dream car from Japan, the UK, or South Africa a seamless and stress-free experience. We handle everything from sourcing to delivery.

Our Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Inquiry: Tell us your desired car specifications.
  2. Quotation: Receive a detailed quote including car cost, shipping, duties, and fees.
  3. Payment: Make an initial deposit or full payment.
  4. Sourcing & Shipping: We source, inspect, and ship your car.
  5. Delivery: Your car arrives in Kenya, is cleared, and delivered to you.
